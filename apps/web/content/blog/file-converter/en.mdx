## FAQ

### What is an HWPX file?

HWPX is the XML-based document format created by Hancom, the dominant office software in South Korea. It is the standard format for Korean government forms, university documents, legal contracts, and business paperwork. HWPX replaced the older binary HWP format and is now required by most Korean institutions.
